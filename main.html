Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Marina Sauce
  • Salt
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil.
  2. Add spaghetti to a boiliing water.
    1. Add salt to taste.
    2. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring ocassionally.
  3. Add marina sauce to a pan, bringing to a simmer.
  4. Mix cooked spaghetti with marina sauce.
  5. Enjoy!
